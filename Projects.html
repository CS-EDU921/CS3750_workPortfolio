Stock Ticker

This is the stock game app where a usesr picks a stock out of the given six options. After picking the stock they will be randomily given a day in the last 2 years and the price of the stock on that day. They are given 10,000 to spend and have to pick how much to buy, sell or hold every round. Each round it an advance of one week from the prevoius price. After ten rounds the user will be forcued to sell all assit for the price of the last rond. From they they will be given a report of how mmuch they started with and the grand total after their weekly trades and holds. 

Self-Task

In this project I was the API designor to build out the toolkit and functions to grab the data from the 3rd party api and formated that data for the teams app. I also designed the logic and storage/transfer tools for the data being sent back and forward from the backend using ajax JS.
